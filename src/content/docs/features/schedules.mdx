The Schedules tab lets you set time windows for automatic charging and discharging. Schedules are written directly to the inverter and run independently of the app — your phone doesn't need to be nearby for them to work.

## Slot limits by device

| Device | Charge slots | Discharge slots |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Gateway | 10 | 10 |
| All In One | 10 | 10 |
| All In One Hybrid | 10 | 10 |
| Hybrid Gen 3+ | 10 | 10 |
| Hybrid HV Gen 3 | 10 | 10 |
| Hybrid Gen 4 | 10 | 10 |
| EMS | 3 | 3 |
| Three Phase Hybrid | 2 | 2 |
| Three Phase AC | 2 | 2 |
| AIO Commercial | 2 | 2 |
| Legacy Hybrid (Gen 1 & 2) | 1 | 2 |
| AC Coupled | 1 | 2 |
| Polar Hybrid | 1 | 2 |

<Aside type="note">
  Legacy Hybrid Gen 3 devices with ARM firmware version above 302 support up to 10 charge and discharge slots.
</Aside>

The Schedules tab shows only the slots your specific inverter supports — you won't see options that don't apply to your hardware.

## Charge slots

Each charge slot has a start time, end time, and a target state of charge. During the slot the inverter will charge the battery from the grid (if solar isn't sufficient) until it reaches that target.

**AC Charge Enable** — some inverters have a master switch that enables or disables charging from the grid entirely, separate from individual slot settings. If your inverter supports this, a toggle appears at the top of the Charge Slots section.

**Example use case:** charge from grid during cheap overnight tariff hours (e.g. Octopus Go, Agile off-peak).

## Discharge slots

Each discharge slot has a start time, end time, and a lower SOC floor. The inverter will discharge the battery during that period but won't go below the floor you've set.

**Example use case:** discharge during peak tariff hours to reduce import costs.

## Export slots *(EMS only)*

EMS devices have a third section for **Export Slots**, controlling when surplus power is pushed to the grid. These follow the same structure as charge and discharge slots.

## Managing slots

- Tap **+** to add a slot
- Tap an existing slot to edit it
- Tap the toggle on the left of a slot to enable or disable it without deleting it
- Swipe left to delete a slot

<Aside type="tip">
  You can disable a slot temporarily using its toggle rather than deleting it. The time and SOC values are preserved on the inverter and re-enabled when you toggle it back on.
</Aside>
