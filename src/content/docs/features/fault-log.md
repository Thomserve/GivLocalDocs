The Fault Log in the Inverter screen shows active and historical faults reported by your inverter.

## Viewing faults

Open **Inverter → Fault Log** to see a list of faults. Each entry shows the fault code and description. Active faults are shown at the top.

## Fault alert banners

When a fault is detected, GivLocal can display an alert banner on the Dashboard. Enable this in **Settings → Dashboard → Fault Alert Banners**.

## Clearing alerts

Once you have reviewed a fault you can clear the alert from within the Fault Log. This dismisses the banner but does not resolve the underlying fault on the inverter.

<Aside type="caution">
  Clearing an alert in GivLocal does not reset or acknowledge the fault on the inverter itself. If a fault persists, contact your installer or GivEnergy support.
</Aside>
